Wooden Cities: Social Improvisation and Action

Wooden Cities presents a series of improvisation workshops with the Youth Orchestra of Bucks County (Philadelphia). Utilizing John Zorn's Cobra, the ensemble will demonstrate free improv practices as students explore various modes of improvisation. Students will also reflect on their role as an artist and music-maker in society and what it means to work together. 10:00am - Wind Symphony workshop 1:00pm - Ripieno workshop 3:00pm - Concertino workshop
